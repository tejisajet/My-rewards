Frequently Asked Questions

How long does activation take? ▼
Activation takes up to 80 hours. Once activated, you'll receive your gift code directly to the email you used during redemption. Your order status will also update to "Activated."
What products can I redeem? ▼
We currently support: $50 Robux Gift Cards, $100 Robux Gift Cards, Minecraft Redeem Codes, and Minecraft Permanent Accounts.
Can I redeem a code that was previously used? ▼
Yes! Per our platform policy, previously redeemed codes can be redeemed again. However, each account must wait for their activation window before reusing a code.
What email will the code be sent to? ▼
Your gift code will be sent to the email address linked to your ThunderVault account. Make sure it's correct when you sign up.
What if I don't receive my code after 80 hours? ▼
Please contact us at elyasgoated@gmail.com with your order ID. We'll look into it and resolve it promptly.
Is my data secure? ▼
Your account data is stored locally and we only use your email for code delivery purposes. We never share your data with third parties.
